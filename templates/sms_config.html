Complete M-Pesa API Setup Guide

  1. Visit https://developer.safaricom.co.ke
  2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
  3. Fill in your details:
    • Full Name
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number (Safaricom Number preferred)
    • Password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Log in to your developer account
Note: You'll need a valid Safaricom phone number for verification.

  1. In your developer dashboard, click "Create App"
  2. Fill in app details:
    • App Name: Water Billing System (or your preferred name)
    • Description: Water billing and payment management system
  3. Select the following APIs:
    • ✅ Lipa na M-Pesa Online (for STK Push)
    • ✅ M-Pesa Express (for payment processing)
    • ✅ Customer to Business (C2B) (for PayBill payments)
  4. Click "Create App"
Important: Make sure to select C2B API for automatic payment tracking!

  1. After creating your app, you'll see it in your apps list
  2. Click on your app to view details
  3. Copy the following credentials:
    • Consumer Key: Found under "Keys" section
    • Consumer Secret: Found under "Keys" section
  4. For testing, you'll also get:
    • Test Shortcode: Usually 174379 (sandbox)
    • Passkey: Provided in the documentation
Sandbox Credentials: Perfect for testing before going live!

  1. Contact Safaricom to request production access:
    • Email: apisupport@safaricom.co.ke
    • Phone: +254 722 000 000
  2. Provide them with:
    • Your business registration details
    • Your app details from the developer portal
    • Your PayBill or Till number
    • This callback URL: {{ request.url_root }}mpesa/paybill_callback
  3. They will provide:
    • Production Consumer Key & Secret
    • Your actual shortcode (PayBill/Till number)
    • Production passkey
Timeline: Production approval usually takes 1-3 business days.

  1. In your Safaricom developer portal, go to your app settings
  2. Configure the following URLs:
    • Validation URL: {{ request.url_root }}mpesa/validation
    • Confirmation URL: {{ request.url_root }}mpesa/paybill_callback
  3. Set Response Type to "Completed" (not "Cancelled")
  4. Save the configuration
Important: Your website must be accessible via HTTPS for production!

  1. Start with sandbox environment
  2. Enable "Use House Number as Account Reference" setting
  3. Test payment with house number as account name
  4. Check if payment is automatically recorded
  5. Verify SMS notifications are sent
  6. Once testing is successful, switch to production
